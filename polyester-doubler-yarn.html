Polyester Doubler Yarn

Two or more yarn plies combined and twisted together for enhanced bulk, strength, and coverage. Produces heavier, more robust fabrics for demanding applications.

End Uses

Applications

Carpet & Rug Yarns

Provides the necessary bulk, weight, and durability required for plush, long-lasting residential and commercial floor coverings.

Heavy Upholstery

Offers excellent coverage and enhanced strength, making it ideal for robust furniture fabrics that withstand daily wear and tear.

Blankets & Throws

Creates a heavier, more substantial feel while retaining softness, perfect for warm, cozy, and durable bedding materials.

Canvas & Tarpaulin

Delivers exceptional tensile strength and structural integrity needed for heavy-duty outdoor covers and industrial tarps.

Webbing & Strapping

Ensures maximum load-bearing capacity and abrasion resistance for safety belts, cargo straps, and heavy-duty narrow fabrics.

Furnishing & Drapery

Produces thick, opaque curtains and drapes that provide excellent light blocking, sound insulation, and a premium aesthetic.

Sports & Outdoor Fabrics

Used in the construction of rugged outdoor gear, tents, and backpacks where heavy denier and tear resistance are critical.

Automotive Textiles

Meets rigorous automotive standards for seating and interior trims, providing high durability and resistance to UV and friction.

Ideal For

Best Suited For

Doubler yarn is the preferred choice for applications demanding heavier fabric weight, superior bulk, and enhanced strength — from plush carpets to heavy-duty industrial fabrics.
