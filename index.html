Company Profile

Rooted in Surat,
Supplying Globally

NEELAM FIBERS (Khodiyar Group of Industries) started the operation in the year 1992 in manufacturing of Texturised Yarn. Later in the Year 2001 went for forward integration in the field of Dyed Yarn production. Presently production achieved up to 5000 MT per annum. Neelam offering outstanding Quality standards in Bright filament, Textured, Twisting, Doubling and High bulk Dyed Yarn to seeking Weaving (Suiting, Shirting, Sarees, Dress Materials) Embroidery, Knitting, Home Furnishing, Tapes,Lables, Carpets, Jari Covering, Shoe Upper, Car Upholstery, Sewing, Threads, etc

Uncompromising Quality & Sourcing: Our analysts maintain strict quality control from raw material procurement to final production. We source premium yarns—including POY, FDY, Bright, Semi-dull, Micro, and Cationic Dye-able—ranging from 30 to 650 Denier in over 1,500 shades.
Advanced Infrastructure: Our modern manufacturing unit is equipped with sophisticated machinery for texturising, TFO twisting, dyeing, winding, and packing. This robust setup also supports our upcoming expansion into new Textile and Medical Textiles, including non-woven products.
Global Distribution: An extensive network of experienced dealers, agents, and depots ensures we efficiently cater to customer requirements across India and internationally.
